About

The CSU-San Marcos Cycling and Triathlon teams are official club sports of the California State University at San Marcos. We welcome athletes at all levels from beginner to elite, and help them reach their endurance sports goals. We encourage participation from undergraduate, graduate, and credential students. We also appreciate the support of the staff, faculty, alumni, and other members of the community. Again, all levels of experience are welcome, so stop by a workout and give it a try!

Membership

The Cycling and Triathlon teams are tiered structure here at CSU-San Marcos. This structure helps our coaches and captains design better training protocols, schedules, and allocate their resources both during and after team workouts. See below for information on the specific tiers.

Check out our calendar to see a schedule of workouts for the season, but be aware in the event of a schedule change, the team may not be at the locations listed on the calendar. The teams have a variety of workouts throughout the year.

Please come try us out if you are at all interested in being part of our program. For information on joining or trying a team practice, check out our ::Membership Page::. Also, join our google group ::San Marcos Racing::.

Club Tier

This membership is geared to the "club athlete" that wants to do some training and racing without strict commitments. This is a great way to keep in shape and pickup a great hobby with no experience neccessary.

Material benefits to joining the program include great discounts from our sponsors, access to club practices and events, discounted uniforms, and race registration support.

Team Tier

The Team Tier is for any team member who plans on aggressively competing in racing and increasing their performance levels. Team tier is is based on commitment to team workouts and development of the entire club. This tier is for dedicated athletes who will attend nearly all training sessions and race a full season of events. Benefits to joining team tier include priority access to funds for training, registration, and travel.

Team applications will be reviewed by the officers and faculty advisors for initial acceptance and team members will be evaluated throughout the season for continued support.

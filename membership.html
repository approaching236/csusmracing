How to join Cycling and/or Triathlon club

Our pre-season base training typically begins in October for the collegiate season in the Spring. If you want to check out the team, you can try out our practices for one week without signing up. However, you must first submit a risk waiver to the ASI recreation club sports office located in The Clarke. You are also welcome to join our ::San Marcos Racing Google Group:: for your trial period. Workout times and locations are on our GOOGLE.CALENDAR. Email your questions to csusmcycling@gmail.com for cycling, tricsusm@gmail.com for traithlon. Or speak to a team officer before or after a workout. Once you have decided to join, follow the instructions below to register.

Criteria, Forms, and Dues

Membership in the CSU-San Marcos Cycling and Triathlon teams are yearly, following the academic semester system, Fall through Summer. All memberships expire at the end of the summer quarter. Email the cycling and/or triathlon team for information regarding membership.

  1. Both new and returning members must fill out ::LINK TO ASI PACKET:: to join the team.
  2. You will need a MANDATORY copy of a valid photo ID (i.e. Student ID, Drivers License etc.) and MANDATORY copy of a valid insurance card.
  3. Pay your dues via check made out to "CSUSM TRIATHLON CLUB" or "CSUSM CYCLING CLUB" and contact a club officer for deposit.

Student Membership

Students (graduate, credential, and undergraduate) receive the full benefits of being part of the CSU-San Marcos Cycling and/or Triathlon Teams. This includes participation in official team and coached workouts, ability to purchase and race in official CSU-San Marcos race gear, eligibility to participate in collegiate race divisions and receive collegiate race discounts, team compensated travel and lodging for particular races, and ASI Recreation Department benefits.

Cost

$100/Team

All team members are required to purchase at least one item of the official team racing gear for that year. Tops run approximately $50; gear will be available for purchase twice a year (TBA).
